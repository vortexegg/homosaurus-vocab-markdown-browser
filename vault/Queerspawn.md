## Description (en)

People with one or more queer parent or caregiver, usually for self-identification. Queerspawn is believed to have been coined in 1995 or 1996 by Stefan Lynch for use in zine published by COLAGE, a U.S. based organization dedicated to supporting people with one or more LGBTQ+ caregiver.
