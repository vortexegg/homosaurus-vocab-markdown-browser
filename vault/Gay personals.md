## Description (en)

A form of classified advertising for gay men interested in seeking connections around sex, romance, and/or community. The content of these ads focus largely on the type of arrangement sought and details or criteria about the writer and the type of person they are looking for (e.g. personality traits, physical looks, etc.). While gay personals are most commonly thought of as existing in print media, they also have existed online in spaces such as Craigslist.
