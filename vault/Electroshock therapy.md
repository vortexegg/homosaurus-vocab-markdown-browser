## Description (en)

A form of psychiatric treatment for major depression, bipolar depression, and other mental health conditions that usually consists of brief electrical pulses to the brain through electrodes on the head that induce controlled seizures. Electroshock therapy has historically been used as an attempt to 'cure' homosexuality and other non-normative sexualities and sexual practices.
