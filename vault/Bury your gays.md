## Description (en)

The trope in fiction that requires that LGBTQ+ characters die or meet some other type of unhappy ending. The trope is also known as 'dead lesbian syndrome' due to the tendency for lesbian characters and bisexual female characters to be the targets. Transgender characters have also often been subjected to this trope.
