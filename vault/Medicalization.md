## Description (en)

Process by which human conditions and experiences come to be defined and treated as medical conditions and thus become the subject of medical study, diagnosis, prevention, and/or treatment. Use in reference to non-medical problems, such as LGBTQ+ identities and practices, that have been wrongly treated as medical issues.
