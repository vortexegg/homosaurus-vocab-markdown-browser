## Description (en)

Work carried out between trained professionals and LGBTQ+ youth,  involving providing affirming, supportive, and culturally competent services to address their unique needs and experiences. This includes supporting their emotional, mental, and physical well-being, as well as advocating for their rights and needs within various systems, including schools, families, and communities.
