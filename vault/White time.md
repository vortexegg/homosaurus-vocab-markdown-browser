## Description (en)

Term used to describe the racialization of time in a white supremacist society, which results in unequal temporal access to institutions, resources, power, etc. This term also references the ways in which non-white people are expected to conform to specific, linear patterns over time, as well as the calculation of time and history through white, Western expectations for progress and productivity.
