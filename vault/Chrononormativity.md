## Description (en)

Term used to describe the ways in which human lives and experiences are expected to follow specific, linear patterns over time in conformity with normative frameworks. This includes expectations regarding how long and what hours people work, with the aim of achieving maximum productivity, as well as expectations for people to reach life milestones, such as graduating high school, getting married, and having children, at specific ages and in a particular order.
