## Description (en)

A genre of fanworks in which two or more characters of the same sex or gender are placed in sexual or romantic situations with one another. Slash fanworks are commonly erotic or pornographic. While the term 'slash' has historically been used for male/male couples only, it has been recently been broadened to include LGBTQ+ relationships other than those between two men.
