## Description (en)

Financial and logistical assistance, often provided by a non-profit organization, to individuals seeking abortions. These funds may also support needs like travel, lodging, childcare, abortion doulas, translation services, and meals, but are not abortion providers. They may also advocate for changes in legislation that restrict access to abortion.
