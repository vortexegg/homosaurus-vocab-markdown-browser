## Description (en)

The homoerotic fan pairing of Kirk and Spock from Star Trek: The Original Series. The Kirk/Spock fandom originated much of the terminology and practices associated with fandom today--for example, the word 'slash' comes from the forward slash in Kirk/Spock.
