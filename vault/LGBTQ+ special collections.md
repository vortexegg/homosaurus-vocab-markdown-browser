## Description (en)

Special collections that hold materials primarily pertaining to LGBTQ+ people or intended for use by LGBTQ+ people. Special collections tend to be a blend of institutional types (e.g., an archives with a museum component). This term should be applied to institutions that refer to themselves as special collections; otherwise, use the term 'LGBTQ+ cultural heritage institutions.'
