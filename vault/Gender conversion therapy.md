## Description (en)

Efforts to change the gender identity of a transgender or non-binary person to meet cisgender norms, typically under the belief that their identity is dangerous and/or the result of mental illness or trauma. The processes involved in conversion therapy include visualization training, spiritual interventions, and other aversion treatments.
