## Description (en)

The practice of traveling with the intention of soliciting sex work, typically to countries where sex work is legal, decriminalized, or less stigmatized. Sex tourism often presents ethical concerns, such as the economic gap between tourists and residents, differences in age of consent laws globally, and the selective enforcement of anti-sex work laws by police.
