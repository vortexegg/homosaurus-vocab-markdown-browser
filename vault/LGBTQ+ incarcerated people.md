## Description (en)

LGBTQ+ people whose liberty is restrained against their will, with or without legal process, whether by a government authority, institution, or extrajudicial actors. Generally, this refers to individuals subject to physical and/or legal confinement within a jail, prison, detention center, etc. It may also include LGBTQ+ people forcibly confined within hospitals and other healthcare facilities.
