## Description (en)

A scenario in which two people are pursuing or are romantically involved with one person, or in which one person is in a romantic relationship while pursuing or becoming involved with another. Often, the two individuals dating, pursuing, or being pursued by the same person are depicted as rivals competing for a monogamous romantic relationship. Love triangles should not be confused with triads, throuples, or other forms of polyamorous relationships.
