## Description (en)

People born with congenital chordee have a penis that curves upward or downward or bends to one side, usually due to bands of fibrous tissue that pull the penis in a particular direction. People with congenital chordee often have hypospadias, meaning that their urethral opening is not at the tip of the penis.
