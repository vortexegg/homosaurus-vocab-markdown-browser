## Description (en)

Groups of drag performers who share a community and typically live in close quarters with one another. Drag families are headed by drag mothers or drag fathers, with other members termed drag daughters and sons. Members of a drag family typically use the same last name as their drag mother or father (e.g., Xtravaganza) in social contexts or while performing.
