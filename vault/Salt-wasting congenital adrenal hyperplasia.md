## Description (en)

People who have salt-wasting congenital adrenal hyperplasia (CAH), a subtype of classical CAH, experience 'salt-wasting,' a dangerous scenario a person's low levels of aldosterone cause their body to lose too much sodium. They may experience life-threatening adrenal crises, especially when their body is under stress.
