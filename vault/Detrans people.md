## Description (en)

People who cease or reverse the social and/or medical process of gender transitioning for any reason, including health concerns, lack of support, economic issues, personal safety, stigma, and a changing understanding of or relationship to their gender. Detransitioning may be temporary or permanent. Detrans people may still identify as trans or under the trans umbrella. Only use for people who self-identify as detrans or detransitioners.
