## Description (en)

Books or other resources of information about places designed for the use of LGBTQ+ visitors or tourists, typically to direct travelers to establishments or attractions that are owned by, open to, or frequented by LGBTQ+ people. LGBTQ+ travel guides may also direct travelers to safe houses or businesses in places where LGBTQ+ people are otherwise unsafe or criminalized, as well as locations commonly used for cruising, or picking up casual sex partners.
