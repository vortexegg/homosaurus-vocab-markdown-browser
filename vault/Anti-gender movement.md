## Description (en)

A movement that opposes what is referred to as 'gender ideology,' or the belief that gender is a social construct that exists on a spectrum. Members of the anti-gender movement oppose the visibility, acceptance, and civil and reproductive rights of trans, non-binary, intersex, Two-Spirit, gender diverse, and gender non-conforming people.
