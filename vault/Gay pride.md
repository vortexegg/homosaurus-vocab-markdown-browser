## Description (en)

The recognition and celebration of gay people and culture, thereby affirming the visibility, dignity, and diversity of gay communities. This term can be used to refer to gay men specifically, but it is often used as an umbrella term for LGBTQ+ pride as a whole, particularly in historical contexts.
