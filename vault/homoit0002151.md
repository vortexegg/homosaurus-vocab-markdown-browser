## Description (en)

Japanese word that means 'beautiful boy' and is used to describe a particular aesthetic of androgynous male beauty. While in Japanese 'bishounen' refers specifically to youths under the age of twenty and 'biseinen' is used to describe beautiful adult men, English-speaking anime and manga fan communities often use 'bishounen' in reference to any good-looking male character, regardless of age.
