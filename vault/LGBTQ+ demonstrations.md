## Description (en)

Marches, rallies, and other large, public gatherings organized to protest discrimination against LGBTQ+ people and advocate for LGBTQ+ visibility, rights, and issues. For street processions of people recognizing and celebrating LGBTQ+ people and culture, use the term 'Pride parades.'
