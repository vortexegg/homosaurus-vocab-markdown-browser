## Description (en)

Identification of LGBTQ+ people, or people believed to be LGBTQ+, by police using digital tools, such as phone records, mobile applications, facial identification algorithms, and artificial intelligence technologies. Such data is then used to entrap and prosecute LGBTQ+ persons, especially in areas where being LGBTQ+ is criminalized.
