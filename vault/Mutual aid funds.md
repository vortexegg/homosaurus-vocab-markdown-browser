## Description (en)

Funds that distribute goods and money collected and raised using the mutual aid organizational model. This model utilizes voluntary, collaborative exchanges of resources and services for common benefit and take place amongst community members to overcome social, economic, and political barriers to meet common needs. This can include funds which distribute resources like food, clothing, or medicine, as well as those that provide financial support for medical services, rent payments, and other financial needs.
