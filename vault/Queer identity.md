## Description (en)

Identity term that can be used to describe several not-necessarily-mutually-exclusive understandings of queerness. Queerness can describe identities that exist outside of or between different identities, and also identities that actively resist normative understandings of sexuality and/or gender. This term should only be used for those who self-identify as queer.
