## Description (en)

A theoretical framework first conceptualized by Kimberlé Crenshaw to describe how different facets of social identity (such as gender, race, ethnicity, disability, socioeconomic class, religion, and sexual identity) overlap and intersect, often compounding the individual's experiences with inequality and discrimination.
