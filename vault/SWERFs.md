## Description (en)

An acronym for 'sex work-exclusionary radical feminists,' this term refers to people who subscribe to a subset of radical feminism that rejects sex worker rights and solidarity with sex workers under the belief that sex work is antithetical to their ideology and inherently perpetuates oppression against women.
