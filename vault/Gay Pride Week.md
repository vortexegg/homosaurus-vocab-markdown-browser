## Description (en)

A period of public recognition and celebration of LGBTQ+ people and culture, usually organized over the course of a week with local events, parades, marches, and rallies. Gay pride week occurs at different times during the year in different places but is typically held in June in the United States to commemorate the Stonewall uprising.
