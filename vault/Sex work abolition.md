## Description (en)

A movement to abolish sex work through a variety of different means, including supporting 'end demand' laws, the Nordic model, or the broader criminalization of sex work; organizing and speaking vocally against sex work as inherently exploitative; and rejecting efforts to recognize sex work as labor.
