## Description (en)

Slang term used to describe men who have romantic and/or sexual relationships with other men; often used as an alternative to the term 'Men who have sex with men.' Can be used to describe men who are exclusively or primarily interested in men, as well as men who are also interested in people of other or all genders. Only use to describe people who self-identify as MLM.
