## Description (en)

The projection of heteronormative ideals and constructs onto LGBTQ+ cultures and identities, predicated on the assumption that LGBTQ+ people (particularly cisgender gay men and lesbians) must conform to the norms and values of heterosexuality (e.g., the gender binary, marriage, procreation, and monogamy) in order to be viewed as 'normal' and gain social acceptance.
