## Description (en)

Quilts created to celebrate the lives of people who have died of AIDS-related causes, bring awareness to the AIDS epidemic, and provide support and healing for those affected. The term is often used to refer to the NAMES Project AIDS Memorial Quilt started by Cleve Jones in 1985.
