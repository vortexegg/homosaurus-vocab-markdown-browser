## Description (en)

A committed polyamorous relationship in which a couple adds a third partner to their relationship. Often, this relationship dynamic does not have a flat hierarchy and the third is seen as dating the couple as a unit or joining the couple's pre-existing relationship rather than individually dating both other members of the throuple.
