## Description (en)

Marches, rallies, and other large, public gatherings organized to protest discrimination against dykes and advocate for dyke visibility, rights, and issues. This term should only be used to describe demonstrations explicitly for lesbians who identify as dykes or that are centered on the dyke identity and politics. Otherwise, use the more general term 'Lesbian demonstrations.'
