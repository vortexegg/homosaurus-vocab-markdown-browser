## Description (en)

The creation of a set of laws and regulations intended to legalize the practice and solicitation of sex work, particularly street sex work and other forms of 'direct,' visible sex work, within certain parameters. This may variously include legalizing the actions of sex workers, their clients, third parties, and/or other individuals who work in proximity to or seek to aid sex workers. Individuals operating outside of these laws and regulations remain subject to penalties.
