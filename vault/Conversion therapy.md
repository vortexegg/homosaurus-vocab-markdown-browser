## Description (en)

Efforts to change the sexual or gender identity of an individual who is or is suspected to be LGBTQ+ to meet heterosexual or cisgender norms, typically under the belief that being LGBTQ+ is dangerous and/or the result of mental illness or trauma. The processes involved in conversion therapy include visualization training, spiritual interventions, and other aversion treatments.
