## Description (en)

An individual's ability to 'pass,' or be accepted or regarded as a member of the gender they identify with. 'Passing' has historically been used to describe an individual's ability to 'pass' as a certain race or ethnicity, though the term was later appropriated by the LGBTQ+ community.
