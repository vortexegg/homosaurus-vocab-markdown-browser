## Description (en)

Romantic, sexual, or play relationships between a masculine-identifying or -presenting person and a feminine-identifying or -presenting person. Though the terms 'butch' and 'femme' are most commonly used in lesbian communities, they are also used by other LGBTQ+ people. Only use to describe relationships between individuals who self-identify as butch and femme.
