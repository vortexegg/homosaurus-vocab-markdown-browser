## Description (en)

The restraint of an LGBTQ+ person's liberty against their will, with or without legal process, whether by a government authority, institution, or extrajudicial actors. Generally, this refers to physical and/or legal confinement within a jail, prison, detention center, etc. It may also include the forced confinement of LGBTQ+ people within hospitals and other healthcare facilities.
