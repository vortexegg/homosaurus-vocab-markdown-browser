## Description (en)

Social, psychological, behavioral, and medical interventions designed to support and affirm individuals belonging to the intersex community. Intersex-affirming care includes but is not limited to equal access to health care facilities, access to medical records and information about one's intersex status and traits, and informed consent regarding medical procedures or treatments related to intersex traits.
