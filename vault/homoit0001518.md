## Description (en)

Japanese term for people who do not fit within the categories of man or woman; includes individuals born intersex, female, or male, and is often abbreviated using FTX, MTX, or XTX to describe individuals' transitions; some definitions equate the term to genderqueer or beyond the binary identities.
