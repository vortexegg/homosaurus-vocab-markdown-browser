## Description (en)

A spiritual movement that emerged in North America, Western Europe, Australia, and New Zealand in 1970s in response to male-dominated organized religion; involves worshiping the Goddess (or several goddesses), as well as a focus on women, femininity, and feminism; often associated with lesbian communities.
