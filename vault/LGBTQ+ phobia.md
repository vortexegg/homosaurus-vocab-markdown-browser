## Description (en)

Antagonism or disgust directed towards LGBTQ+ identities or people due to their sexuality or gender, perceived or actual. LGBTQ+ phobia can include denial of the existence of certain LGBTQ+ identities, stereotypes, verbal harassment, and physical violence.
