## Description (en)

Term originating in Rabbinic Judaism to refer to someone with an unknown sex; literally translates to 'hidden.' Used historically to refer to intersex variations where the genitals were covered by skin. Only use for individuals who are Jewish and who self-identify as Tumtum.
