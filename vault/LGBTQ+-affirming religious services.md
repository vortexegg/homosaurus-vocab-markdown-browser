## Description (en)

Religious services, or gatherings of people belonging to the same religious group or belief system, that are accepting and supportive of LGBTQ+ people and their identities. Includes services specifically intended for LGBTQ+ people within a denomination or sect that is otherwise not LGBTQ+-affirming.
