## Description (en)

A form of classified advertising for asexual people interested in seeking connections around sex, romance, and/or community. The text of these ads focus largely on the type of arrangement sought and details or criteria about the writer and the type of person they are looking for (e.g. personality traits, physical looks, etc.). While asexual personals are most commonly thought of as existing in print media, they also have existed online in spaces such as Craigslist.
