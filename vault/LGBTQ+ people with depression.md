## Description (en)

LGBTQ+ people who experience any of the key symptoms of depression, including but not limited to persistent sadness, loss of interest in everyday activities and difficulty and managing work, relationships, etc. LGBTQ+ people are at risk for a higher rate of depression due to life factors such as social stigma, discrimination, lack of family support, and other stressors.
