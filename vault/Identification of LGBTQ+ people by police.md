## Description (en)

Identification of LGBTQ+ people, or people believed to be LGBTQ+, by police using any of various tools and mechanisms, many of which are pseudoscientific in nature. Such data is then used to entrap and prosecute LGBTQ+ persons, especially in areas where being LGBTQ+ is still criminalized.
