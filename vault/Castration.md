## Description (en)

Removal of the testes or use of the testes, typically through surgical or chemical means. Includes castration voluntarily undertaken as a gender-affirming surgical procedure or as part of a religious rite. For involuntary castration enacted by legal authorities or as a hate crime, use the term 'Forced castration.'
