## Description (en)

Networks of polyamorous people who are connected by their or their partners' romantic and/or sexual relationships. Polycule members are not necessarily romantically and/or sexually involved with every member of the group. For example, Member A might be connected to Member B in that Member B is in a relationship with Member A's partner.
