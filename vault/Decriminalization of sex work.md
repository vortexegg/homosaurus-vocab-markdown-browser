## Description (en)

The process of lessening or removing laws, regulations, and/or criminal penalties targeting the practice, solicitation, and/or facilitation of sex work. Decriminalization efforts may variously address the actions of sex workers, their clients, third parties, and/or other individuals who work in proximity to or seek to aid sex workers.
