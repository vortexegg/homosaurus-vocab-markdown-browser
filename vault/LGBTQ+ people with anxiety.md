## Description (en)

LGBTQ+ people who experience any of the key symptoms of anxiety including but not limited to feelings of unease, worry and fear, commonly as a reaction to stress. LGBTQ+ people are at risk of higher rates of anxiety due to life factors such as social stigma, discrimination, lack of family support, and other stressors.
