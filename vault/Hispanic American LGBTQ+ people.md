## Description (en)

LGBTQ+ people who were born in or currently reside in the United States and are descended from Spain or other regions formerly colonized by Spain that are primarily Spanish-speaking. Only use for individuals who self-identify as Hispanic American; do not conflate with Latino/a/x or Chicano/a/x.
