## Description (en)

Efforts to propose, enact, amend, or strike down laws as a means of restricting the legal rights of transgender people. This includes legislation regarding access to gender-affirming healthcare, participation in sporting events, administrative and judicial procedures such as sex and name registration, and policies affecting the medical treatment, education, and mental health care of transgender youth.
