## Description (en)

The practice of transgressing or 'bending' expected gender roles, which often involves adopting or exaggerating stereotypically feminine or masculine traits to expose them as artificial. Gender bending is similar to drag, though drag is often associated with artistic or stage performances, whereas gender bending is typically enacted in everyday life.
